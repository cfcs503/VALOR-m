VALOR-m was developed using LASSO-penalised Cox regression in a population-based cohort of 33,462 older adults with VLOSLP (CDARS, Hong Kong, 2002–2024) and validated via spatiotemporal internal-external cross-validation. Cause-specific models use Fine-Gray competing risks regression. This tool is intended to support clinical decision-making and does not replace clinical judgement. For research and clinical use only.
